Work at JavaJam

Barista

As a Barista, you'll be responsible for crafting delicious coffee and providing excellent customer service. Your tasks will include:

  • Preparing espresso-based drinks
  • Steaming milk and creating latte art
  • Interacting with customers to take orders and offer recommendations
  • Maintaining cleanliness of the coffee bar and equipment

Hourly Wage: $12.50

Cashier

The Cashier is responsible for processing customer payments efficiently and ensuring a smooth checkout experience. Your tasks will include:

  • Taking customer orders and accurately entering them into the system
  • Handling cash and credit card transactions
  • Providing receipts and addressing customer inquiries
  • Keeping the cash register area organized

Hourly Wage: $11.00

Shift Supervisor

As a Shift Supervisor, you'll oversee daily operations and ensure the team's efficiency. Your tasks will include:

  • Assigning tasks and responsibilities to team members
  • Monitoring inventory levels and placing orders as needed
  • Addressing customer concerns and resolving issues
  • Opening and closing the coffee shop as scheduled

Hourly Wage: $15.00

Pastry Chef

The Pastry Chef is responsible for creating and maintaining a delectable selection of pastries and desserts. Your tasks will include:

  • Baking and decorating a variety of pastries, muffins, and cakes
  • Ensuring the freshness and quality of baked goods
  • Managing inventory of pastry ingredients
  • Collaborating with the kitchen team to develop new recipes

Hourly Wage: $14.50

Cleaner

The Cleaner plays a crucial role in maintaining a clean and hygienic coffee shop environment. Your tasks will include:

  • Regularly cleaning and sanitizing all areas of the coffee shop
  • Emptying trash bins and maintaining restroom cleanliness
  • Restocking cleaning supplies as needed
  • Assisting with general maintenance tasks

Hourly Wage: $10.00
